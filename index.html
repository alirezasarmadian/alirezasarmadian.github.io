OBJECTIVE

I am a Chancellor's-Award-winning PhD student and a Doctoral Tutor at the University of Sussex. As my PhD thesis, I am working on an industrial funded project with Ford, Ricardo, and Denso with a focus on an evaporative spray cooling system proposed for cooling automotive electrical and electronic powertrain components. After completing my Doctoral Programme, my ultimate goal is to pursue a career in an academic area, for I want to play a useful role to influence my research field. Therefore, after graduation, I would like to continue my research in pioneer companies or academia.
